Announcing dahliaOS 201004!

Noah Cain - October 4, 2020

    That's right, after months of work, 201004 is finally here! The team has worked hard adding in a ton of new features and functionality! This time around, we've added in a whole new way to multitask, improved settings, added a new version of the system for users with EFI-based computers, and laid some of the groundwork for future networking!  However, keep in mind that this is still in development, and is not yet complete. Here’s what’s new:

Take control of the system (in style!) with Settings

From the beginning, giving users freedom over every aspect of the system has been a major goal for us. With the new and improved Settings app, every component of the system can be modified and tweaked to your liking. With the new Settings app, everything from developer tools to customization is at your fingertips.

Customization

With the new Customization page in Settings, you can choose through various options to tweak the system to your liking. Make dahliaOS your own with a custom layout, theme color, and more!

Dark Mode

Are you tired of harsh light themes? We certainly are, that’s why we have gone ahead and added in a new Dark Mode! Perfect for night, indoors, or wherever you need it!

Internationalization

With the new Languages setting, dahliaOS now adapts to you. In the coming months, we hope to support as many languages as possible! Know a language that we are missing? Anyone can help us translate the system at translate.dahliaos.io!

A new way to multitask

We have now added simple window snapping, so you can get things done more efficiently! Simply drag the window using the directional arrows, and they will snap into place, side by side, up and down, anywhere!

A new and improved base

Under the hood, we have worked tirelessly to get the system running as efficiently as possible. In dahliaOS 201004, we have significantly decreased memory usage (almost 100MB less than the previous build!), cleaned up unnecessary kernel drivers, and added in filesystem persistence! (EFI builds only)

Improved Performance

dahliaOS 201004 is a perfect system for low spec devices, like low-spec tablets and ultralight laptops! By decreasing the memory usage, now you have plenty of memory for all your apps, without crashes.

Deep into the system...

In 201004, we have added in some new kernel drivers and dropped some others. In the “hardware only” EFI image, we have removed unnecessary drivers that allow it to integrate with the host within a virtual environment, and added in new drivers for common networking devices.

In Conclusion

We hope you enjoy this release as much we did making it! Keep in mind that not everything is finished yet, but thanks to our team and community contributors, we are getting closer and closer! You can download and try it today from the links below!
